1. Get the gRPC service endpoint:

   kubectl get svc {{ include "rehydration-kernel.fullname" . }}

2. Override connection settings for Neo4j, Valkey, and optional NATS through:

   - values under `connections`
   - or `secrets.existingSecret` for credential-bearing URIs

3. The chart deploys the standalone kernel service. Product adapters and
   product-specific event bridges stay outside this chart.
